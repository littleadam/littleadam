## Leadership Philosophy

> *"The Director title is awarded by organisations. The Director presence is built one decision at a time — in the room, in the email, in the trade-off conversation no one else wants to have."*

Three principles that govern how I lead:

**1. Outcomes over activities.** Every team update, status report, and sprint review should answer: what did we deliver, what does it mean for the commitment, and what — if anything — requires a decision. Not what we worked on.

**2. Problems arrive with options.** I do not escalate problems to stakeholders. I escalate decisions — with the problem framed, the options assessed, and my recommendation clear. The stakeholder's job is to choose, not to analyse.

**3. Systems over individuals.** When something goes wrong, I look for the process gap, not the person to blame. Every incident, every missed sprint goal, every quality escape is an opportunity to build a system that prevents the next one.
